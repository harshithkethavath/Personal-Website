In Progress 2024–25

WeatherMind MLOps

Taking a research prototype and making it actually work in production. WeatherMind is a multi-model VLM weather analysis system rebuilt as a production-grade async inference service — FastAPI front, Celery/Redis task queue, Kubernetes orchestration, Terraform-provisioned, with Prometheus and Grafana watching everything.

Built locally on Minikube, written to be AWS-ready from day one. The point isn't the weather — it's what it takes to ship ML reliably.

MarsHazardNet

A multi-task U-Net that simultaneously classifies terrain hazards and segments surface features from Mars Context Camera imagery. Two outputs, one shared encoder — the hypothesis being that joint training produces richer representations than either task alone.

Due April 2026. Metrics will be added once complete.
